THE HIGHER EDUCATION YOUTH INTIATIVE AND EMPOWERMENT (THEYIE)

Registered under sector 10 of the Non-Governmental Organization Coordination Act, THEYIE is a one-of-a-kind NGO that seeks to positively impact the lives of higher education youth and empower them to transform their lives for the betterment of society at large.

THEYIE, through its various programs and initiatives, aims to provide educational opportunities, skill development, and mentorship to young individuals, enabling them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

With a focus on fostering leadership, promoting social responsibility, and instilling a sense of civic duty, THEYIE strives to create a generation of empowered and engaged citizens who are equipped to address the challenges facing our world today. .

NATIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TRANSFORMATION

ICT

ICT (Information and Communication Technology) has emerged as the cornerstone of most socio-economic spaces, driving innovation, efficiency, and connectivity across various sectors. Within THEYIE, the ICT department is effectively steered by students pursuing ICT courses in our higher education programs.

The primary objective of this department is to ensure that basic ICT knowledge is accessible to even the most marginalized individuals. By providing training, resources, and support, we empower individuals to harness the power of technology for personal and professional growth

Community Service

This department at THEYIE focuses on empowering and promoting the welfare of marginalized and vulnerable groups. Its primary mission is to reach marginalized and vulnerable members of society by providing essential necessities such as food, clothing, and hygiene materials, while also advocating for their rights and addressing their needs.

Through targeted outreach programs, community engagement, and advocacy efforts, the department aims to uplift and support those facing socio-economic challenges, fostering inclusivity and social justice within the community

Enviroment

Rallying youths to be the forefront of environment conservation efforts

Youths who account for about 80% of Kenya's population are the most effective in advocating for environmental conservation .

Their passion, energy, and innovative ideas drive positive change in addressing environmental challenges, making them vital contributors to sustainable development efforts.

Education

Promoting access to education involves providing, developing, and advancing frameworks for improvement and facilitation of educational spaces. This includes initiatives to motivate and empower individuals to pursue learning opportunities.

Ensuring that education is accessible to all, irrespective of socio-economic status or geographical location we can create an inclusive educational environment that empowers individuals to unlock their potential and contribute meaningfully to society's progress and prosperity.

Agriculture

With the empowerment and professionalization of agriculture, we aim to bolster the sector's contribution, which currently accounts for 28% of our GDP and domestic product output. Approximately 30% of the country's total labor force is employed in agriculture, highlighting its significant role in our economy
