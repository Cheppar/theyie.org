Youth Empowerment:

The High Education Youth Initiative Empowerment (THEYIE) empowers through mentorship, workshops, training, and networking, fostering academic and career success.THEYIE equips young adults with skills and confidence for the future. Inclusivity ensures equal opportunity, creating a supportive environment where every voice matters. THEYIE is not just a program; it's a transformative journey toward brighter horizons.

GOAL:

Promote Access to Education, mitigate the impact of drug/substance abuse, contain the spread of HIV/AIDS and promote the interests and welfare of youth and other marginalized groups.

MISSION:

To unite, protect and empower the Youth and marginalized groups to be productive citizens in the society they live in and with secure livelihoods.

VISION:

An inclusive and empowered society where all citizens including the youth contribute to development and the society's wellbeing.

VALUES:

Inclusiveness

We value diverse views and strive to build a robust and open organization where varied perspectives are respected and maximised for a positive outcome.

Professionalism

We are professional in delivering our mission with integrity. We operate in a culture of trust with one another as well as with our constituents.

Boldness

We are bold in seeking innovative ways to do our work, and in communicating our ideas. We strive to always do the right thing.

Commitment

We are committed to provide high training skills in all who are need.
